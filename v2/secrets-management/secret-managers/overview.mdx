---
title: "Integration with Secret managers"
---

For users of Bruno Ultimate, you can take your secret management one step further and perform an integration with a secret vault. 

Currently, we support integrations with:

- [HashiCorp Vault](/v2/secrets-management/secret-managers/hashicorp-vault/overview)
- [AWS Secrets Manager](/v2/secrets-management/secret-managers/aws-secrets-manager/overview)
- [Azure Key Vault](/v2/secrets-management/secret-managers/azure-key-vault/overview)
